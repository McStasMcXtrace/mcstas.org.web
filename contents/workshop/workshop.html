Workshops and Conferences

Here is a list of past events, relevant to McStas users:
  • McStas Workshop, January 2001, Risø (Denmark). A report is given here in Microsoft Word format.
  • Workshop on VITESS 2.0 and other Packages for Simulations of Neutron Scattering 25-27 June 2001, Hahn-Meitner-Institut Berlin (Germany). The talks of the workshop are available at the Vitess website.
  • SINS-II Workshop - SImulation of Neutron Spectrometers, march 20-22th 2002, ILL, Grenoble, France. Program and proceedings available.
  • SCANS network meeting, October 2002, Abingdon (UK). The slides presented by Peter Willendrup and Emmanuel Farhi are available in html and pdf format.
  • SCANS network meeting, April 2003, Prague (CZ). The slides presented by Peter Willendrup and Emmanuel Farhi are available in html and pdf format.
  • Joint VITESS and McStas Training Course and Workshop at ISIS, Dec 11th, 2003. Read more at the workshop website.
  • SPIE conference in Denver, Aug 2004: contributions to the AM304 session.
  • NOBUGS 2004 conference at PSI, Oct 2004.
  • McStas at ACNS 2006, June 2006
  • International Workshop on Applications of Advanced Monte Carlo Simulations in Neutron Scattering, Oct 2006: Tutorial and many McStas talks.
  • ICNS 2009 - check out our own page of McStas related work and Workshop page.
