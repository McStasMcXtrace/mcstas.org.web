Authors and contacts

If you have any questions or comments about McStas, please contact the McStas developers at <mcstas-support@mcstas.org>.

If you have any questions or comments of general interest, send them to the Monte Carlo mailing list <mcstas-users@mcstas.org>. More information on this list and how to subscribe are available at this link.
