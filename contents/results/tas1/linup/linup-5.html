Investigation of error in collimator line-up

To investigate the possible cause of the 3.6' offset of the peak in the measured PHM scan with collimation, the effect of slightly misaligning C1 was investigated. The following table shows simulated peak offset for various values of OMC1. Instrument definition linup-2.instr.

OMC1 Peak offset
-1.8' 0.9'
-5' 3.2'
-6' 4.0'
-10' 7.1'

We see that the 3.6' peak offset can be attributed to a mis-alignment of C1 of (5.5-1.8)' = 3.7'. This appears quite likely, as C1 is aligned manually in the measurements. We continue simulations with OMC1 = -5.5'.
