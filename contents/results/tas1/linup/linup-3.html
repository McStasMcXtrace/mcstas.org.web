Line-up of M-S collimator

Measurements

Mount 30' collimator C1 between monochromator and sample, otherwise as previous setup. Let OMC1 denote the angle of rotation of C1 around the Y (vertical) axis. C1 is manually aligned by scanning OMC1, choosing the angle of maximum intensity.
