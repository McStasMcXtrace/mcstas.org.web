2T scan on 1mm slit

Measurements

Now the monitor is put in the proper position just after collimator C1, and the rest of the measurements are done using the single detector in the analysator house. The 10.5x40.5 slit is kept on the sample position, but an 1x40mm is placed 30mm behind, and atenuators are placed before the detector. The instrument is run in 2-axis mode with no analysator. 2T is scanned from -1.2 to 1 for 5 sec/point. Data file linup013.dat.
