Goals and tasks

The goals and tasks of this tutorial are
  • To teach you about the most basic neutron scattering.
  • To let you understand some of the typical components in a neutron scattering instrument.
  • To teach you basic usage of the McStas neutron simulation package.
  • To let you create your first McStas instruments, a two axis diffractometer and a triple axis spectrometer.
  • To teach you how to modify your instrument for a specific task.
  • To help you learn to debug instruments.
  • To help you aquire and analyze data from McStas simulations.
