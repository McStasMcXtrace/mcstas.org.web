The Powder1 component

General powder sample with a single scattering vector. Example: Powder1(radius=0.015,h=0.05,q =1.8049,d_phi0 =0.07,pack=1,j=6,DW=1, F2=56.8,Vc=85.0054,sigma_a=0.463,target_x=0,target_y=0, target_z=1000)

Input parameters

Parameters in boldface are required; the others are optional.
Name Unit Description Default
radius m Radius of sample in (x,z) plane  
h m Height of sample y direction  
q AA^-1 Scattering vector of reflection 1.8049
d_phi0 deg Focussing angle corresponding to the vertical dimensions of the detector placed at the right distance 0.07
pack 1 Packing factor 1
j 1 Multiplicity of reflection 6
DW 1 Debye-Waller factor of reflection 1
F2 fm^2 Structure factor of reflection 56.8
Vc AA^3 Volume of unit cell 85.0054
sigma_a fm^2 Absorption cross section per unit cell at 2200 m/s 0.463
target_x 0
target_y m position of target to focus at 0
target_z 1000
