The Monitor component

Simple single detector/monitor.

Identification

  • Author: KL
  • Origin: McStas release
  • Date: October 4, 1997
  • Version: 1.6

Description

Sums neutrons (0th, 1st, and 2nd moment of p) flying through 
the rectangular monitor opening. May also be used as detector.
