Documentation

Available documentation:
  • The The McStas tutorial.
  • The McStas manual.
  • A list of Frequently asked questions (FAQs) (with answers).
  • Documentation of the McStas Component library.
  • A document (postscript (.ps) format, 160 Kb) describing an interface between the TASCOM control program and McStas on the Risø spectrometer RITA-1.
  • Documentation on McDoc, the McStas automatic documentation generator.
  • A list of known bugs in McStas.
  • A draft of the American standard for writing components for neutron simulation, in PDF format (10 Kb).
  • The Conditions of use for McStas.
  • A report (in Danish) on the results of testing a Compaq Alphaserver DS10 as a computation server running on-line McStas simulations of current neutron instrument configurations.
  • A report on a first simulation of polarised neutrons, by Trefor Roberts <roberts@ill.fr>. The simulation is very basic, but nevertheless privides a good starting point for doing polarised neutrons in McStas.
  • A note on compiling simulations generated by McStas; the choice of compiler and compilation options can make a real difference (more than a factor of two in some cases!).
  • A note on the problem with buggy compilers with McStas - the HP ANSI C compiler and the PGCC compiler produce incorrect results with full optimization!
  • A document describing the first prototype of McStas, available in a4 postscript, dvi, and pdf formats.
  • A page describing a facility for graphic debugging of instrument definitions.
