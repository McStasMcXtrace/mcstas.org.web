Dear fellow simulators,

First of all, I would like to thank the participants of the January McStas
workshop. It was a most valuable workshop for the McStas crew - and
hopefully for the participants also. 

Following one of the discussions at the Workshop, I have had a
conversation of the editor of Nuclear Instruments and Methods, B (NIMB),
Prof. H.H. Andersen. He supported the idea of publishing neutron
simulation related manuscripts in this journal. So I will hence propose to
you all to consider publishing in NIMB. (Of course the papers will be
referee'd after usual standards - we get nothing free.)

Yours,

Kim
