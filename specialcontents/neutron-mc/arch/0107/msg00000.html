Hello neutron-mc members,

The ILL list of available instrument simulations performed with McStas has been extended today.
They include particularly:

  1. The TOF-TOF simulation by S. Roth at FRM-II with helium sample, various vanadiums, a radial oscillating collimator, etc...
  2. The ROG reflectometer by S. Rycroft at IRI with a multilayer reflecting sample
  3. A general curved guide that has been used for the ILL7 guide hall study (see reports)
  4. The IN5 ToF spectrometer by J. Ollivier, with a pre report
  5. The D20 diffractometer simulation by T. Hansen (there is now a link to the web pages, even if the work was done some time ago)
  6. The H8 TAS spectrometer at Brookhaven (The McStas simulation, to be compared to the ResTrax, NISP and Vitess simulations)
These simulations are provided as starting points for other neutron-mc member simulations, and components included inside may be re-used elsewhere.
Anyway, please keep informed the author of the simulations when you work on their stuff. I will, when I get time, tidy up and filter all this in order to extract components that can easely be re-used elsewhere, and may become part of the McStas distribution.
All files are given without any guaranty that it will run for your own simulation. Source code may (and will) be somehow hieroglyphic, but that's what we got.

Link: McStas@ILL library <http://www.ill.fr/tas/mcstas/ill_lib.html>
