Dear McStas user,

It is now possible for McStas users to upload McStas-related files to a
public directory on our web-server (http://neutron.risoe.dk/mcstas),
which then can be downloaded by other users. As discussed at the McStas
workshop two weeks ago, it cannot only be used for unofficial components

and instruments but also for other kinds of relevant material.

To get some minimum level of protection against misuse of the service,
a userid, MCstas, and password, mcSTAS, are required, where both are
case-sensitive. Let us hope that it works, the alternative would be to
use individual userids and passwords.

Best regards,

Per-Olof Åstrand
