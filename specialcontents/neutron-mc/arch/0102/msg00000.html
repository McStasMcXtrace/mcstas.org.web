After some requests at the McStas workshop I talked to Kurt Clausen about
the possibility to have MCMP output for input to instrument MC simulations.
Apparently there will be a new person employed by the ESS project whose
probable first task will be to do just that for the suggested ESS
moderators. It may be possible to sort out some details on this at either
the ESS instrumentation group meeting in February or SAC meeting in March.
I will let you know. Presumably one thing is to agree suitable formatting
etc. For calculations from moderators for existing reactor sources it will
take some more work to find the right people.
