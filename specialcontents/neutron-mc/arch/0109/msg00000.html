Dear McStas users,

I have cleaned up in my component directories before the 1.5 release. This
resulted in 5 "new" monitor components, that I have found useful during
the last two years. Note that there is a big overlap with Emmanuels
monitor_nD, which covers all but the last of my monitors.

PSDlin:     Rectangular, 1D, I vs. position
EPSD:       Rectangular, 2D, I vs. position and energy
PSDcyl:     Cylindrical, 2D, I vs. position (angle and height) 
TOF_cylPSD: Cylindrical, 2D, I vs. time and position (angle)
TOFlog:     Rectangular, 1D, I vs. time (logarithmically binned)

Yours,
