directory). Basically, the problem is that the fortran library has been
renamed from libf2c to libg2c in recent Linux distributins. I changed
all instances of "f2c" to "g2c" in the makefile, and after that "make
test" seemed to go better; I do not have an X display here in Oxford,
though, so I cannot check myself!
