I was also thinking to a way of speeding up computations.
At the begining, for each neutron coming out of the source, you store its state with a probability of 1 in an emission table. A quick mapping of neutrons could be done as default starting table.
If during neutron-ray tracing, it is ABSORBed, you decrease it's probability in emision table (till a lower limit). For new neutrons coming out of the source, you respect the emision probability if it is near an older stored neutron, or you add its characteristics to the emission table.
If a neutron reach the detector, you raise its emission probability.

It seems quite simple to implement, what do you think ?
Also, did you think about grouping components into a compound component ?
